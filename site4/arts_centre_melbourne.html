  • Arts Centre Melbourne

This could be Australia's first CMS. It's a definite contender for Australia's first large-scale online design system.

The internet looked very different in 1998 and AustraliaPost was already getting onboard; providing customers with information once only available at their local post office. From 1998-2001 I was in charge of the site UI and oversaw its design as it became one of Australia’s favourite websites. I created a design style, a reusable template system for the data-driven CMS and countless ‘mini-sites’ for special events and products.

Client:Arts Centre Melbourne

Date:2014

Agency:Citrus

Site:www.austpost.com.au
